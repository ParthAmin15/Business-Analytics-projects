# 📊 Business Performance & KPI Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an **Executive-Level Business Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze key business metrics such as revenue, profit, growth, and customer behavior.

The objective of this project is to provide **data-driven insights** and **actionable recommendations** to support strategic decision-making at the management level.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to monitor overall business performance due to fragmented data and lack of centralized reporting. This leads to:

* Poor visibility into revenue and profit trends
* Inefficient decision-making
* Missed growth opportunities

This project aims to solve these challenges by building a **centralized KPI dashboard**.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Revenue shows consistent growth over the years
* Technology category contributes the highest profit
* Furniture category has lower profit margins
* Profit trends show fluctuations despite increasing revenue
* Revenue is concentrated in specific regions
* Few sub-categories (e.g., Phones, Copiers) drive majority of sales
* Low repeat purchase behavior observed among customers

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize pricing strategy in low-profit categories
* Focus on high-performing categories like Technology
* Expand into underperforming regions
* Improve customer retention through loyalty programs
* Prioritize high-revenue products
* Monitor and stabilize profit fluctuations
* Leverage seasonal demand patterns

Problem Statement
The retail organization was experiencing inconsistent sales performance, declining profitability in specific categories, and limited visibility into product-level performance. Management required a structured reporting system to monitor KPIs like Sales, Profit, and Regional trends.

🛠 Tools Used
Excel / Google Sheets: Pivot Tables, KPI Dashboard creation, Conditional Formatting, Data Visualization.

Business Analysis: KPI definition, Process flows, and Root Cause Analysis.

📊 Key Findings
Regional Performance: The West region generates the highest sales, followed by the East, Central, and South regions.

Category Insights: Technology products lead in profit margins, while Furniture and Office Supplies require inventory optimization.

Profitability Alert: Bookcases, Tables, and Supplies were identified as "Loss Products," requiring immediate attention to pricing or supply chain costs.

KPI Dashboard: Developed a centralized view tracking Total Sales ($2.29M+), Total Profit, and Average Discount rates.

NOTE:- Project is done online in google spreadsheets

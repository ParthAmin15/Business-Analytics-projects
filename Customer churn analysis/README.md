# Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy

## Project Overview

The **Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy** project focuses on identifying the key factors contributing to customer attrition in a telecom business environment. The objective of the project is to analyze customer behavior, measure churn patterns, and propose data-driven strategies to improve customer retention and support business decision-making.

This project follows an **end-to-end Business Analyst approach**, including requirement analysis, data analysis, dashboard development, and stakeholder-oriented documentation.

An interactive Tableau dashboard was developed to visualize churn trends and customer segments, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance and implement retention strategies.

---

## Business Problem

Telecom companies often face customer churn, which leads to revenue loss and reduced customer loyalty. Management requires visibility into customer behavior and churn drivers to make informed decisions and improve retention strategies.

The organization needs a structured analysis to:

* Identify customers at risk of churning
* Understand factors driving churn
* Monitor churn trends using dashboards
* Develop strategies to improve customer retention

---

## Project Objectives

* Identify key drivers of customer churn
* Analyze customer demographics and subscription behavior
* Calculate churn-related KPIs
* Develop an interactive Tableau dashboard
* Provide actionable retention strategies

## Key Business Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate
* Customers with **short tenure** are more likely to churn
* Higher monthly charges increase churn risk
* Certain payment methods show higher churn behavior
* Customer churn directly impacts revenue stability

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following retention strategies were proposed:

* Promote long-term contracts through discounts and incentives
* Improve customer onboarding and early engagement
* Introduce flexible pricing and bundled services
* Encourage automatic payment methods
* Implement customer loyalty programs

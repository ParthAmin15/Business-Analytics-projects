# HR Analytics — Employee Attrition & Workforce Planning

## Project Overview

The **HR Analytics — Employee Attrition & Workforce Planning** project analyzes employee data to identify key factors influencing employee turnover and provide actionable strategies to improve workforce retention.

This end-to-end Business Analyst project covers the complete analytics lifecycle — from data cleaning and KPI analysis to dashboard creation, documentation, and business recommendations.

The objective of the project is to help organizations reduce attrition, improve employee satisfaction, and support data-driven workforce planning decisions.

---

## Business Problem

High employee attrition leads to increased recruitment costs, reduced productivity, and operational inefficiencies. Organizations require data-driven insights to understand attrition patterns and implement effective retention strategies.

This project aims to:

* Analyze employee attrition trends
* Identify key drivers of employee turnover
* Monitor workforce metrics
* Support HR decision-making
* Improve employee retention

## Key Insights

* Higher attrition observed among employees working overtime
* Employees with low job satisfaction show increased turnover risk
* Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave
* Certain departments experience higher attrition rates
* Younger employees demonstrate higher mobility

---

## Business Recommendations

* Implement employee engagement and recognition programs
* Reduce excessive overtime workload
* Introduce mentorship and onboarding support
* Monitor department-level attrition trends
* Use dashboards for workforce planning decisions

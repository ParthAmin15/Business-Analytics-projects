🚗 Electric Vehicle (EV) Market Intelligence
Integrated Analysis of Sales, Infrastructure, and Consumer Trends
Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive market intelligence dashboard for the Electric Vehicle (EV) industry. By integrating sales performance metrics with consumer search interest data, this analysis bridges the gap between what is selling and why it is trending.

Key Datasets
Sales & Infrastructure Dataset: Contains monthly EV unit sales, charging infrastructure growth, and gasoline price indices.

Consumer Trends Dataset: Contains monthly search trends for EVs (Tesla, BYD, Charging stations, etc.) across various global markets.

Key Insights
The "Infrastructure" Effect: Strong correlation found between charging station availability and EV sales growth, validating that infrastructure is a leading indicator for adoption.

Price Sensitivity: Demonstrated negative correlation between gasoline price fluctuations and EV adoption rates.

Market Dynamics: Identified top-performing brands (BYD, Tesla) and mapped market maturity using a Growth-Share Matrix.

Trend Analysis: Analyzed how search interest for "EV Charging" and specific brands correlates with historical sales spikes.

Methodology
Data Synthesis: Merged market sales data with trend scores to identify leading indicators for EV adoption.

NOTE:- Project is done online in google spreadsheets.

Correlation Modeling: Used statistical analysis (CORREL) to test the impact of external drivers (Gas price, Infrastructure) on consumer purchase decisions.

Momentum Analysis: Tracked Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and rolling averages to identify market acceleration patterns.

Tools Used
Excel / Google Sheets: Pivot Tables, Data Correlation, Growth-Share Matrix Visualization, Trend Analysis.

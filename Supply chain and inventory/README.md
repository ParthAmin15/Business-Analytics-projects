# Supply Chain Performance & Inventory Optimization Analysis

## Project Overview

The Supply Chain Performance & Inventory Optimization Analysis project focuses on analyzing supply chain operations to improve inventory management, supplier performance, and logistics efficiency. The project leverages data analysis techniques to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify operational risks, and provide actionable business recommendations.

A comprehensive dashboard was developed to visualize supply chain performance metrics such as revenue, stock levels, lead time, shipping time, and defect rates. The project also includes complete Business Analyst documentation such as BRD, FRD, process flow diagrams, activity diagrams, use case diagrams, user stories, and UAT.

This project demonstrates end-to-end Business Analysis capabilities, from data cleaning and KPI analysis to stakeholder-focused documentation and business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often face challenges in managing inventory and monitoring supplier performance due to limited visibility into operational data. These challenges can lead to:

* Overstocking and stock shortages
* Delayed shipments
* Increased operational costs
* Poor supplier performance tracking
* Inefficient decision-making

The objective of this project is to build a data-driven system to monitor supply chain performance and support operational decision-making.
